How to Tutorials

Change Engine Oil

User:Admin

1 - Identify what oil you need and how much.
2 - Gather your supplies (Oil, Filter, Catch Pan, Wrenches and/or Sockets, Rags, Any Specialty tools.
3 - Set parking brake, chock tire, jack up car and support with proper stands.
4 - Place pan under the plug and filter, remove drain plug then undo filter and allow both to drain.
5 - Clean any excess oil and replace filter and drain plug.
6 - Add new oil then check level once the car is on flat ground.
7 - Record mile and date for the next change interval.

Painting Tips

User:Admin

The best thing to do is strip the car down to bare metal. Then do all the bodywork (Keep any fillter to a maximum of 1/8 inch). Block sand the entire car making several passes from alternating directions (Be sure to use guide coat). Then spray the final coat of primer followed by sealer. At this point you can do 2-3 coats of base coat followed by 3-5 coats of clear. Then cut and buff.

Change Spark Plugs

User:Admin

Best practice is to change one plug at a time so that you dont mix up the wires. Gap the plugs to spec (Most come pre gapped). Put a thin coat of anti-seize on the thread then torque to spec.
