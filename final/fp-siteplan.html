Site Name

Classic Car Connection

Site Purpose

Allow me to introduce myself. My name is Robert Odell and I have a great idea for a website. Do you like classic cars? Well, you’re not alone. Thousands of people out there own a classic car, or perhaps they are just fans of them. I am proposing a website where people can come and learn about classic cars. There will be different sections based on decade, style, and models. Not only will you learn the history of each car, but you can also chat with other car owners and buy or sell cars or parts. Don’t you think this is a great idea?

Target Audience & Scenarios

My target audience will include the following

  1. Classic Car Enthusiast
  2. Classic Car Owners
  3. Peolple looking to Buy/Sell cars
  4. People looking to chat or get advice

Site Map and Content

Classic Car History

This page will include history of different classic cars. It will be grouped into decades. I would like to tell the history and significance of the decade and include some vehicles to showcase. For the initial release of the site there will be a small number of vehicles, but I would like to design the page to easily grow as new cars are added. There can be a different car showcased each month.

Buy and Sell

This page will be designed to display a listing of vehicles for sale. The plan is to allow users to post their cars for sell like a classified ad. Users can also post classic car parts for sell of ISO ads to look for those hard-to-find parts.

Discusion Board

This page will be a place where you can search for post in a discussion board. The topics can range from general chat to DYI tutorials and writeups. This will most likely be a nonfunctioning design at first to show the concept. Post will be moderated to limit improper post or behaviors to provide a wholesome and family friendly atmosphere. For the initial release of the site this will be a basic how-to and information page.
