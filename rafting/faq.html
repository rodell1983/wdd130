About Us

What safety equipment is provided?

We provide only the highest grade safety equipment for our trips. Every item is inspected before and after each trip and adhears to all safety regulations

What happens if I get hurt?

All of our guides are trained in CPR, First Aid and BLS. We have a very good track record and make every effort to keep you safe.

Do I need to sign a waiver?

Yes, It is very standard to make clients sign a liabity waiver. You do have the option to purchance insureance for any unlikely events.

What is your safety record?

We have one of the best safety record in the industry. Rest assure, you are in great hands

How much is an average trip?

Take a look at our trip planner page. Trips start at around $50 all the way up to $1,000+ on average, 1 day trips are $50 - $100. 3 day trips are 400-500, and 1 week trips are $1,000.

How much is insurance?

Insurance is arount $20 per day.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes. We offer discounts for military, first responders, seniors, and other promotionals.

When is payment due?

Payments are required in full prior to the trip.

How long have you been in buisness?

We have been in buisness for over 50 years

How did you start out?

Ros river rafting was started over 50 years ago by local Idaho rafting enthusiast. It has remained a family owned buisness ever since.

Are you affiliated with any other rafting companies?

No. We are an indipendent, localy owned, family buisness.
