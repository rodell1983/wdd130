Site Name

Robs River Rafting

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote Robs River Rafting and allow users to explore the services that we provide. Robs river rafting provides a wide range of excurtions from the beginners level to the extreme adreneline junky. We offer trips from 1-2 hours up to multi day endurance trips. We encurage groups as such family reunions, buisness trips, church trips, youth groups and so on. On this site the user can learn about river rafting, book a rafting trip, and purchse rafting products. Also they can purchase personalized products and memorabilia. The goal of this site are to get people intersted in rafting and to have them want to book a trip with us. Also to make it an easy, plesent, and user friendly experience.

Target Audience & Scenarios

My target audience will include the following

  1. First Time Rafters
  2. Families
  3. Groups
  4. Experienced Rafters

Scenarios will include the following

  • The website will attempt to detect the device and adjust the layout if needed.
  • Users can pick excursions based on their experience level
  • Users can pick excursions based on price
  • Discounts offered based on the group size
  • Allow users to view maps of where the different trips are taken
  • Allow users to purchase merchandise
